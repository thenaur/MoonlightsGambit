## РђДРѓі╦џ Рћі Overview
**Moonlight's Gambit** is a 4-player social deduction game set in the ancient academy of Artemia, where players discover hidden loyalties through magical mechanics and tragic romance. Souls bound by love and friendship must navigate secrets that could destroy everything they hold dear.

In this game of hidden allegiances, players take on fantasy roles with unique abilities, all while concealing their true intentions. Will love survive duty? Will friendship endure betrayal? The masks are on... let the tragedy unfold.

## Рюд Рћі Features

- **Four Unique Characters**: Each with special night abilities and compelling backstories
- **Dynamic Game Phases**: Rotating night actions, dawn revelations, and voting sessions
- **Tragic Romance System**: Special interactions between lovers Elara and Orion
- **Friendship Mechanics**: Unique choices for childhood friends Calisto and Luna  
- **Strategic Social Deduction**: Blend of role abilities and player deception
- **Destiny Draw Duel**: Final confrontation mechanic with risk-reward decisions
- **Immersive Storytelling**: Rich narrative with multiple endings based on player choices
